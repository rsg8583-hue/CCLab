Plastic Waste in Our Oceans

An interactive simulation showing the growing problem of plastic pollution

Year: 2025

Plastic Objects in Ocean: 0

Each year, between 11 and 12 million metric tons (or up to 26 billion pounds) of plastic waste enter the ocean, equating to roughly 2,000 truckloads daily, with most coming from land via rivers, breaking down into trillions of tiny pieces that pollute surfaces and seabeds, harming marine life. While estimates vary, scientists agree the volume is massive and growing, with 5.25 trillion pieces already estimated to be floating in the ocean.
